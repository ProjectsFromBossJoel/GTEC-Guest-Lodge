01

Introduction

Welcome to GTEC Guest Lodge. We are committed to protecting the personal information of our guests and users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and safeguard your data when you use our online booking platform at gtecguestlodge.web.app.

By using our services, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please refrain from using our platform.

02

Information We Collect

When you use our booking platform, we may collect the following:

  • Identity information: Full name and email address
  • Contact details: Phone number
  • Booking details: Check-in/check-out dates, room selection, number of guests, and purpose of stay
  • Authentication data: Google account information if you sign in with Google
  • Usage data: Pages visited, actions taken in the app, and browser type
  • Communications: Messages sent via our live chat feature

Payment Information: We do not collect or store credit card or payment information directly. Any payment processing is handled by trusted third-party providers under their own privacy policies.

03

How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected solely to operate and improve our services:

  • Process and manage your room reservations
  • Send booking confirmations and updates via email and SMS
  • Authenticate your identity through Google Sign-In
  • Respond to live chat and support inquiries
  • Generate booking confirmation letters and invoices
  • Improve the functionality and experience of our platform
  • Comply with legal and administrative obligations

04

Google Sign-In

Our platform uses Google OAuth 2.0 to allow you to sign in securely with your Google account. When you use this option, we receive only your name and email address from Google. We do not receive or store your Google password.

Your use of Google Sign-In is also governed by Google's Privacy Policy. You may revoke our app's access to your Google account at any time through your Google account settings.

05

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With service providers who help operate our platform (e.g., Google Firebase, EmailJS) under confidentiality agreements
  • When required by Ghanaian law or a valid legal process
  • To protect the rights, safety, or property of GTEC Guest Lodge or its users
  • With GTEC administrative staff strictly for booking management purposes

06

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase, which employs industry-standard encryption and security measures including data encryption at rest and in transit.

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes outlined in this policy, or as required by law. Booking records are retained for administrative and legal compliance purposes.

While we implement strong security measures, no online system is completely immune to risk. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password and keep your account credentials secure.

07

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of any inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Withdrawal: Withdraw consent for data processing at any time
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details in Section 11.

08

Cookies

Our platform may use cookies and similar technologies to maintain your session, remember your preferences, and improve your experience. These are small text files stored on your device by your browser.

You can control or disable cookie settings through your browser preferences. Please note that disabling cookies may affect certain features of the platform, such as staying signed in.

09

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has submitted personal information to us, please contact us immediately and we will promptly delete it.

10

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our services or applicable law. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date at the top.

We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of our platform after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
